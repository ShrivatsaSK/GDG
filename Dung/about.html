About Us

We blend tradition with technology to revive India’s native cow breeds. Using IoT sensors and AI, we monitor cow health and dung quality in real time—improving nutrition, detecting diseases early, and promoting sustainable farming. Our mission is to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that empowers farmers and preserves our heritage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What breeding technologies are you developing?
We're combining traditional selective breeding approaches with advanced genetic analysis and AI-driven predictive models. Our technology aims to identify optimal breeding pairs for desired traits, monitor reproductive health in real-time, and provide data-driven recommendations to maximize successful outcomes and herd quality.
How does your cow dung processing system work?
Our proprietary processing system will involve several stages of decomposition, filtration, and refinement. We're developing both aerobic and anaerobic processes to break down organic matter efficiently, extract valuable nutrients, and eliminate pathogens. The end result will be clean, odorless products that retain all the beneficial properties for agricultural use.
Are your solutions environmentally friendly?
Absolutely! Environmental sustainability is at the core of our mission. Our breeding technologies aim to reduce resource consumption while improving herd health. Our waste management systems transform what would otherwise be a pollutant into valuable resources, reducing methane emissions and water contamination. All our products are designed with ecological principles in mind.
How can farmers benefit from your solutions?
Farmers can benefit in multiple ways: improved breeding outcomes lead to healthier, more productive herds; our waste management systems create new revenue streams from existing resources; and our organic products can reduce dependency on expensive chemical inputs. Overall, our solutions aim to increase profitability while promoting sustainable farming practices.
